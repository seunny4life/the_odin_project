Nigeria Fried Rice

Fried Rice served with Dodo and fried chicken

Fried rice is the second most popular food enjoyed in Nigeria after Jollof rice

They are mostly served with fried meat or fried chicken and they must be served warm to enjoy the sweetness of the food.

Nigerian fried rice is typically cooked with liver and prawns, but these could be substituted with other proteins.
