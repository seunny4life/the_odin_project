Jollof Rice

Jollof Rice served with Dodo and fried chicken

Jollof Rice is the most commonly served meal at any Nigerian party.

They are mostly served with fried meat or fried chicken and they must be served warm to enjoy the sweetness of the food.
The best part of this meal is the one prepared at the Owambe parties.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Fresh Tomatoes
  • Curry powder
  • Fresh red peppers
  • Sunflower oil
  • Maggi
  • Thyme leaves
  • Canned tomato paste
  • fresh Red Peppers
  • Red Onions
  • White Onions
  • Habanero pepper(Spicy)
  • Salt
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Blend tomatoes, red bell peppers, habanero peppers and red onions until consistency is smooth and set aside.
  2. Wash the rice thoroughly to rid of starch and set it aside.
  3. Pour the vegetable oil and butter into a medium-sized pot and put medium heat.
  4. Throw in chopped onions and allow to fry for about 3 mins until redness starts to fade.
  5. Pour in the tomato paste and allow to fry for about 10-15 minutes but stir it continuously to avoid burning.
  6. Add in your blended mixture, crayfish, Maggi, white pepper, curry powder, and thyme.
  7. Mix and fry for about 30 minutes, continue to stir to avoid burning.
  8. Add salt
  9. Mix and allow cook for an additional 10 mins
  10. Add the washed rice, a bit of water, and reduce heat to low.
  11. Cover pot with aluminum foils, to avoid heat from escaping through lid.
  12. Cook rice on low heat for about 30 mins.
  13. Once cooked, mix thoroughly to combine ingredients.
  14. Cover and let cook for an additional 10 mins.
  15. Turn up the heat and let the rice burn for about 2 mins.

Food is ready
